Welcome To Learn Pass Drive

Services

Here at Learn Pass Drive, we aren't all about just driving. The services we provide are:

Driver Training

At Learn Pass Drive School of Driving, I offer friendly, patient driving instruction to equip you not only with the skills needed to achieve your driving license but to provide you with a lifetime of safe, defensive driving. My teaching method is easy and clear to follow with techniques that are simple to remember. I am a member of the Professional Driver Trainers’ Association and I carry the appropriate driver training insurances.

Refresher Training

If you are returning to driving, or simply want to improve your knowledge of road laws and maneouvres, Learn Pass Drive School of Driving offers Refresher Driver Training. Refresher training is also ideal for anyone who may be unfamiliar with current Australian road law.

Basic Vehicle Maintenance

Buying and maintaining a vehicle can be a tricky experience. I also offer training in vehicle maintenance which can be incorporated into your driving lesson or booked as an additional lesson.

About Learn Pass Drive

My name is Angelo and I am a licensed Motor Vehicle Driving Instructor, providing driving lessons throughout the Adelaide metropolitan area under the Vehicle On Road Test (VORT) System. My aim is to teach you the necessary skills to achieve your “P’s” and prepare you for a lifetime of safe, competent driving in a friendly, no pressure environment.

Lesson Information

Driving lessons through Learn Pass Drive School of Driving are tailored to suit your level of learning. I cover the five slow speed manoeuvres, associated road law and the general drive of the Practical Vehicle On Road Test. I provide driving lessons in the Western suburbs, Eastern suburbs, City and North East areas.

Learn Pass Drive offers affordable rates and flexible lesson times including early bird lessons from 6am. New and nervous drivers welcomed. To find out more, click on the Driver training, Refresher Driver Training or Basic Vehicle Maintenance pages.
